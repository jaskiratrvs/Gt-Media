Who We Are

Based in London, UK, GT Media ME is a highly respected publisher of business intelligence annuals covering the European, Middle East and North African markets. We produce beautiful, bespoke publications on behalf of the region’s most powerful and influential stakeholders, promoting their interests at home and abroad.

Established in 2007, GT Media ME has quickly forged a reputation for providing unrivalled business intelligence and insight into the European, Middle East & North African markets. Based in London and with a broad regional presence in key markets in the EU and the Gulf, GT Media ME publishes specialised business journals in English and Arabic. Covering booming regional markets including travel & tourism, energy, education, healthcare and transportation, GT Media works with some of the region’s most important stakeholders, producing beautifully designed, bespoke products that showcase the ambitions of this most dynamic, influential market. GT Media ME also provides a platform for foreign businesses, from SMEs to multinational corporations, to promote their products and services to these influential decision-makers. Our years of experience in the Gulf markets makes us the ideal conduit for international companies looking to gain a foothold and raise their profile in the region.

GT Media also publishes the Arabic-language luxury-lifestyle publication, Ain Almusafer, providing insight into the latest global and regional high-end consumer, travel and lifestyle trends.
